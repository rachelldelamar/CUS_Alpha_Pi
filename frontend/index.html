The Org

About Chi Upsilon Sigma

Chi Upsilon Sigma National Latin Sorority, Inc. was founded on April 29, 1980 at Rutgers University. As the first Independent Latin sorority.

Leadership

We are dedicated to the pursuit of excellence, embodying the mission of empowering women and inspiring future CUS sisters to achieve success. Through discipline, respect, and commitment, we cultivate leadership by uplifting our members, fostering wisdom, and creating a lasting, positive impact on those around us.

Sisterhood

Bound by the sisterhood of Corazones Unidos Siempre, we forge lifelong connections built on love, respect, open communication, and shared sacrifice. We strive to nurture unbreakable bonds that transcend time and distance, strengthening one another through unwavering support and unity.

Service

We are deeply committed to serving and educating others, as well as continuing our own growth through civic engagement and community organizing. With intentional action, we uplift underrepresented women, enrich our communities, and make meaningful contributions to our campuses and our organization.

Character

We honor the vision and Code of our Founders by upholding the highest standards of integrity, humility, and professionalism. Guided by our values, we strive to lead with thoughtfulness, authenticity, and a commitment to give our best selves to others.
